House of Code.

UI // UX // web design

The task.

To create a digital redesign for House of Code, a full-service app agency located in Odense. The redesign had to optimise the UX of their page “Start dit projekt”, meet and exceed the expectations of the users and be implementable to House of Code.

The challenge.

The challenge wasn’t so much coming up with a brand new design and identity for House of Code – they already have that pretty much nailed. It was more utilising this design on the “Start dit projekt”-page because at the starting point, this subpage was kind of a bummer to land on compared to the amazing design House of Code otherwise has.

Our solution.

With the page being pretty text-heavy and not super engaging, our solution centred on three focus points, that would optimise the UI and UX of the page: Increasing accessibility, decreasing confusion and adding fun interactions for users.
